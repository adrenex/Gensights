Text annotation is the process of adding information or metadata to textual data to enhance its understanding, relevance, or usability. This additional information can include labels, tags, or other types of annotations that provide context, meaning, or structure to the text. Text annotation is widely used in natural language processing (NLP), machine learning, and data analysis applications to train models, improve search results, and make sense of unstructured text data.

YouTube Video Transcription refers to the process of converting spoken words in a YouTube video into written text.
